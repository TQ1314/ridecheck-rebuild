E) INTERNAL SCORING ALGORITHM (Simple but real)

Create lib/ridechecker/scoring.ts.

Score per job (0–100):

Checklist completeness: 40 points (all required present)

Photo count & clarity proxy: 20 points (min 4 required + extras)

Text completeness: 20 points (min length thresholds per section)

Timeliness: 20 points

if submitted within X hours of scheduled_end: full points

else partial

Store the score on the assignment and update ridechecker aggregates:

avg_quality_score = rolling average

jobs_completed++

on_time_pct updated

No public display. Ops can view.
